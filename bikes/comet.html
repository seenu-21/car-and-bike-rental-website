Product Description

The Bajaj comet 150 is a popular and iconic motorcycle known for its powerful performance and sleek design. It's equipped with a 149.5cc, single-cylinder, air-cooled engine that delivers a maximum power of 13.8 HP and excellent torque for a thrilling riding experience.

This bike is designed for both daily commuting and occasional long rides, making it a versatile choice for riders. Its comfortable seating, powerful headlamp, and stylish look add to its appeal.

Product Features

  • Engine: 149.5cc, Single-cylinder, Air-cooled
  • Maximum Power: 13.8 HP
  • Transmission: 5-speed manual
  • Front Brake: Disc
  • Rear Brake: Drum
  • Comfortable Seating for Rider and Pillion

Additional Notes

The Bajaj comet 150 is a reliable and affordable choice for motorcycle enthusiasts. It offers a great balance of power and comfort, making it suitable for a wide range of riders.
